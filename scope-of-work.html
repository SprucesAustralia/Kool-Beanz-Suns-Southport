Scope of Work

Outdoor Areas
Tasks:
- Walk through and remove all cobwebs
- Glass/window cleaning
Tools:
- Broom
- Glass cleaner
- Microfiber cloths
Staff Room and Reception Area
Tasks:
- Dusting and wiping down furniture, tables, and countertops
- Cleaning and sanitizing communal appliances (microwaves, refrigerators)
- Sweeping, vacuuming, or mopping floors
- Emptying trash bins and replacing liners
Tools:
- Dusters
- Cleaning cloths
- Vacuum cleaner
- Mop and bucket
- Disinfectant
Laundry
Tasks:
- Dusting and wiping down furniture, tables, and countertops
- Cleaning and sanitizing communal appliances (microwaves, refrigerators)
- Sweeping, vacuuming, or mopping floors
- Emptying trash bins and replacing liners
Tools:
- Dusters
- Cleaning cloths
- Vacuum cleaner
- Mop and bucket
- Disinfectant
Prep Rooms and Bathrooms
Tasks:
- Cleaning and sanitizing sinks, countertops, mirrors, and fixtures
- Refilling soap dispensers, toilet paper, and paper towel holders
- Sweeping and mopping floors
- Disinfecting toilets and urinals
- Emptying and sanitizing trash bins
Tools:
- Disinfectant
- Cleaning cloths
- Mop and bucket
- Scrub brushes
Library and STEM Room
Tasks:
- Dusting and wiping down shelves, tables, and chairs
- Vacuuming or mopping floors
- Organizing books, educational materials, and resources
Tools:
- Dusters
- Vacuum cleaner
- Mop and bucket
Classroom Cleaning
Tasks:
- Pick one classroom per day
- Dusting and wiping down all surfaces (desks, chairs, shelves, whiteboards)
- Vacuuming or mopping floors
- Disinfecting toys, play equipment, and high-touch surfaces
- Emptying trash bins and replacing liners
Tools:
- Dusters
- Vacuum cleaner
- Mop and bucket
- Disinfectant
Additional Tasks
Tasks:
- Periodic cleaning of windows and glass surfaces
- Adhering to all health and safety guidelines and protocols
- Flexibility to assist with other cleaning duties or tasks as assigned
Tools:
- Glass cleaner
- Microfiber cloths
- Safety equipment as required
